The Best Movies According to Kris

My top 3 movies of all time.

Terminator

A cybernetic organism time travels from 2029 to 1984 Los Angeles; the cyborg is an assassin known as a Terminator, disguised as a man and programmed to hunt and kill a woman named Sarah Connor.

John Wick

An ex-hitman comes out of retirement to track down the gangsters who killed his dog and stole his car.

Mission Impossible

An American agent, under false suspicion of disloyalty, must discover and expose the real spy without the help of his organization.
